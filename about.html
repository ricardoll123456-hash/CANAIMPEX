Who We Are

20 Years Moving Côte d’Ivoire’s Harvests to the World

Cana Impex is a privately held export house specialising in cashew, cocoa, hevea, and coffee. From our base in Abidjan we partner with cooperatives, processors, and logistics leaders to deliver compliant cargo to roasters, traders, and industrial buyers worldwide.

Rooted in Côte d’Ivoire, Trading with Confidence

We combine origin-side quality control with export documentation, finance, and logistics support. Our bilingual teams guide partners through procurement, sampling, and shipment execution from Côte d’Ivoire’s ports of Abidjan and San Pedro.
