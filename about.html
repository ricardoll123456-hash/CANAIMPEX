Who We Are

20 Years Connecting Côte d’Ivoire to Global Markets

Cana Impex is a privately-held export house built by agronomy and logistics experts. From Abidjan, our team manages end-to-end sourcing for cashew, cocoa, hevea, and coffee buyers who demand quality, compliance, and reliable sailings.
