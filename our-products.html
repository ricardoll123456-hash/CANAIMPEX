Product Portfolio

Premium Agricultural Commodities from Côte d’Ivoire

We combine origin-side expertise, quality control, and reliable logistics to deliver cashew, cocoa, hevea, and coffee that meet the specifications of roasters, processors, and industrial clients worldwide.

Cashew

Raw Cashew Nuts & Kernels

Sourced from Côte d’Ivoire’s northern belt, our cashew program provides consistent volumes with calibrated grading and humidity control. We support both raw cashew nut (RCN) shipments and processed kernel supply.

  • •Grades: W240, W320, LP, and custom specifications.
  • •Processing: BRC-certified plants with steam sterilisation.
  • •Packaging: 25 lbs vacuum tins or 80 kg jute bags.
Cocoa

Export-Ready Cocoa Beans

We partner with farmer cooperatives to secure traceable, high-quality cocoa. Each lot undergoes cut tests, moisture checks, and sensory evaluation prior to shipment.

  • •Quality: Well-fermented, 7.5% max moisture, <10% slaty.
  • •Packaging: 62.5 kg jute bags with liner options.
  • •Certifications: Conventional, UTZ, Rainforest Alliance upon request.
Hevea

Natural Rubber (Hevea Brasiliensis)

Our hevea supply chain focuses on cup-lump and TSR production with strict FFA monitoring. Dedicated field teams support tapping schedules and coagulum collection to maintain consistent grades.

  • •Grades: TSR 10, TSR 20, and concentrated latex.
  • •FFA: Maintained below 0.05% at origin with rapid evacuation.
  • •Packaging: 35 kg bales wrapped & palletised, or drums for latex.
Coffee

Robusta & Arabica Coffee

From high-altitude Man to the volcanic soils of West Africa, our coffee network aggregates both washed and natural profiles, cupped to match your roast requirements.

  • •Origins: Côte d’Ivoire, with neighbouring origins available for blends.
  • •Processing: Fully washed, honey, and natural.
  • •Packaging: 60 kg jute bags with GrainPro liners on request.

Quality Assurance

Every shipment is accompanied by SGS or Bureau Veritas inspection reports, detailed photos, and moisture certifications.

Logistics & Incoterms

We handle FOB Abidjan, CFR, and CIF arrangements with preferential rates from Abidjan and San Pedro ports.

Documentation

Full export packs include phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and traceability dossiers tailored to your market.

Need tailored specifications?

Share your cup scores, out-turn requirements, or rubber specs. Our origin team will prepare matched samples and a shipment roadmap within 48 hours.
